Who We Are

A Bridge Between Buyers and Chemical Suppliers

PureLink Chemicals was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: make chemical procurement transparent, efficient, and fair for businesses of all sizes.

As a B2B chemical marketplace based in Egypt, we connect buyers with a curated network of verified chemical suppliers from across 15+ countries. Our platform uses a unique double-blind bidding system that ensures unbiased pricing, letting the quality and value of each offer speak for itself.

Whether you're a manufacturer seeking bulk industrial chemicals, a lab needing analytical reagents, or a company sourcing specialty formulations — PureLink is your single point of contact for finding the right supplier at the right price.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower businesses with smarter chemical sourcing by providing a trusted, transparent marketplace that connects buyers with verified suppliers worldwide — reducing costs, saving time, and ensuring quality at every step.

Our Vision

To become the leading B2B chemical marketplace in the MENA region and beyond — setting new standards for procurement transparency, supplier accountability, and technological innovation in the chemical industry.

Our Journey

How PureLink Grew

From an idea to a growing marketplace serving businesses across the region.

2021

The Idea

Identified the gap in chemical procurement — fragmented suppliers, opaque pricing, and inefficient sourcing processes. PureLink was conceived as the solution.

2022

Platform Development

Built the core marketplace platform with double-blind bidding, supplier verification, and end-to-end procurement management tools.

2023

Market Launch

Launched in Egypt with an initial network of 20+ verified suppliers covering fine chemicals, specialty chemicals, and industrial products.

2024

Regional Expansion

Expanded our supplier network to 50+ verified suppliers across 15+ countries. Introduced reverse auctions and enhanced analytics for buyers.

2025

Growing Strong

500+ chemical products listed, serving businesses across the MENA region with plans to expand into broader global markets.
