What We Do

How We Simplify Chemical Procurement

From sourcing to delivery, PureLink Chemicals manages every step of the procurement process so you can focus on what you do best.

Service 01

Chemical Sourcing

Finding the right chemical at the right price shouldn't be a headache. Our sourcing team leverages a network of 50+ verified suppliers across 15+ countries to find exactly what you need.

  • Access to global supplier network
  • Multi-supplier price comparison
  • Custom synthesis and formulation sourcing
  • Rare and hard-to-find chemical procurement
Service 02

Procurement Management

Our platform streamlines your entire procurement workflow — from RFQs and quotations to purchase orders and delivery tracking.

  • Double-blind bidding for unbiased pricing
  • Automated RFQ management
  • Purchase order generation and tracking
  • Budget approval workflows
Service 03

Supplier Verification

Every supplier in our network goes through a comprehensive verification process to ensure they meet our quality and reliability standards.

  • ISO and GMP certification verification
  • Production capacity assessment
  • Regulatory compliance checks
  • Ongoing performance monitoring
Service 04

Logistics Coordination

We manage the complete supply chain from the supplier's warehouse to your facility, including documentation and customs clearance.

  • International shipping management
  • Customs documentation and clearance
  • Hazardous materials handling
  • Real-time shipment tracking
Service 05

Quality Assurance

Quality is non-negotiable in the chemical industry. Our QA processes ensure every product meets your specifications before it ships.

  • Certificate of Analysis (COA) verification
  • Batch testing and purity confirmation
  • Full traceability documentation
  • SDS and regulatory documentation
The Process

From Enquiry to Delivery in 4 Simple Steps

Search

Submit your chemical requirements — product name, CAS number, specs, and quantity.

Compare

We source quotes from multiple verified suppliers. You compare anonymized offers side by side.

Order

Accept the best quote. We generate purchase orders, handle payments, and confirm with the supplier.

Deliver

We manage logistics, customs, and documentation. Track your shipment until it arrives at your facility.
